Biography

I am now a postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University under the supervision of Professor Jennifer Dy. I am also a Research Fellow in Brigham and Women's Hospital of Harvard Medical School. I had my Ph.D. training under the guidance of Professor Alexandre Bouchard-Côté in the Department of Statistics at University of British Columbia in December, 2018.
